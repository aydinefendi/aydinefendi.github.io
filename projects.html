VoiceNote.ai

It's a SaaS which enables you to take notes, automate tasks and store your recodrings using voice. Using OpenAI's Whisper API you can transcribe your audio, and using GPT-4o you can chat with an AI bot to ask questions related to the contents of the audio and take actions.
